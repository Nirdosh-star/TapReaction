Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Reflex.GG ("we", "our", "the site") respects your privacy and is committed to being transparent about how the site operates. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit Reflex.GG, how it is used, and what choices you have.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information. Your gameplay data stays on your own device. The only external service we use is Google AdSense for advertising.

Information We Do Not Collect

Reflex.GG does not collect or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your IP address (we do not log server requests)
  • Your gameplay scores, session data, or performance history
  • Your device information, browser version, or operating system
  • Any form of behavioural tracking or analytics data

We do not use Google Analytics, Hotjar, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or tracking service.

Local Storage (Your Device Only)

The games on Reflex.GG use your browser's built-in localStorage API to save your high score, best streak, and play count between sessions. This data is stored entirely on your own device. It is never sent to any server, never shared with any third party, and is accessible only by this site within your own browser. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or local storage.

Google AdSense and Advertising

Reflex.GG uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. This is how the site generates revenue and remains free for all users. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve advertisements based on your interests, your prior visits to this website, and your prior visits to other websites.

How Google Uses Data for Advertising

Google's advertising services use cookies — small text files stored in your browser — to help them serve relevant ads. For example, if you have recently searched for sports equipment, Google may serve ads related to sports on sites you visit, including this one. This is called interest-based or personalised advertising.

Google uses the DoubleClick cookie and similar technologies across its advertising network to track which ads you have seen, which ones you have clicked, and to measure advertising effectiveness. Reflex.GG has no access to or control over these cookies once Google sets them.

Opting Out of Personalised Advertising

You have several options to opt out of personalised advertising from Google:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings to manage your personalised ad preferences across Google's network.
  • Visit aboutads.info to opt out of interest-based advertising from multiple networks including Google.
  • Visit networkadvertising.org for additional opt-out options.
  • Install a browser extension that blocks tracking cookies.

Note that opting out of personalised advertising does not mean you will see no ads — it means the ads you see will not be personalised based on your interests or browsing history.

More Information About Google's Advertising Practices

For a full explanation of how Google uses data from partner sites, visit: How Google uses data from sites or apps that use Google's services.

For Google's full privacy policy, visit: Google Privacy Policy.

Cookies

Reflex.GG itself does not set any cookies. The only cookies present on this site are those set by Google AdSense for advertising purposes, as described above. These are third-party cookies — they are set by Google, not by us.

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies via your browser settings. Blocking all cookies may affect the advertising you see but will not affect any core functionality of Reflex.GG's games.

Children's Privacy

Reflex.GG's games are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to this site, please contact us and we will take appropriate action. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, there is no specific child data to delete, but we take this responsibility seriously.

Third-Party Links

This site may contain links to external websites (for example, links to Google's opt-out pages or news articles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the site's features, advertising partners, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
