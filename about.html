About Reflex.GG

What Is Reflex.GG?

Reflex.GG is a collection of free, lightweight browser-based games designed to test and improve reaction time, hand-eye coordination, and cognitive speed. Every game on this site runs entirely in your web browser — no downloads, no installations, no account creation required. You open the page, press Start, and you are playing within seconds.

The core philosophy is simple: good games do not need to be complicated. Some of the most effective training tools for reaction speed are also the simplest — a target appears, you click it, and you try to do it faster and more consistently than before. That loop, repeated deliberately over time, produces measurable improvements in real-world reaction speed.

Who Made This?

Reflex.GG was built and is maintained by Laxman Koirala, a developer with an interest in browser-based gaming, web performance, and making useful tools accessible to everyone for free. The site started as a personal project and grew from there.

Laxman built Reflex.GG because most reaction time tools online are either too complicated, filled with intrusive ads that interrupt gameplay, or locked behind paywalls. The goal was to create something cleaner: genuinely useful games with a fast, honest user experience, supported by non-intrusive advertising so that everything stays free.

The Games

Tap Reaction is the first and currently active game on the site. It presents a glowing circular target at a random position in the game field. Your job is to click or tap it as quickly as possible. Each hit moves the target to a new random position. The game runs for 30 seconds. Consecutive hits build a streak that multiplies your points — hitting 10 in a row gives you 6 points per hit instead of 1. The streak resets if you miss, which means accuracy matters as much as raw speed.

Two additional games are in development. Speed Tap will measure your raw Actions Per Minute (APM) with no time limit, giving you a precise measure of your clicking speed and how it compares to benchmarks for casual players, regular gamers, and competitive esports players. Memory Flash will challenge your working memory by asking you to reproduce increasingly long sequences of target positions — a different kind of cognitive challenge that complements pure reaction speed training.

How the Site Is Built

Every page on Reflex.GG is built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no large dependencies, no external runtime requirements. This keeps the site extremely fast to load, which matters both for user experience and for search engine ranking. Games run at a stable frame rate in any modern browser, on any device from a budget Android phone to a high-refresh-rate gaming monitor.

Score data — including your personal best, session play count, and best streak — is stored entirely on your own device using the browser's built-in localStorage API. Nothing about your gameplay or behaviour is sent to any server. Reflex.GG does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or session recording. The only external service is Google AdSense for advertising.

How the Site Makes Money

Reflex.GG is completely free for users and is supported entirely by Google AdSense display advertising. When you visit a page, Google may show contextually relevant advertisements. These ads generate a small amount of revenue per visitor, which covers hosting costs and supports continued development of new games and content.

No game feature is locked behind a payment. There is no premium tier, no "remove ads" subscription, and no in-game currency. The advertising is kept as non-intrusive as possible — ads appear in designated zones (top of page, sidebar, below the game) and do not interrupt gameplay or pop up unexpectedly.

The Mission

The mission of Reflex.GG is to provide genuinely useful, well-made browser games to anyone who wants to test or improve their reaction speed — for free, forever. Whether you are a competitive gamer looking to sharpen your aim response, an athlete wanting to maintain sharp reflexes, or simply someone who is curious how fast their reactions actually are, Reflex.GG is built for you.

We believe that free should not mean low quality. Every game on this site is built with care, tested across devices, and designed to give you a fair, accurate, and enjoyable experience every time you play.
