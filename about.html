About

What is Reflex.GG?

Reflex.GG is a collection of free browser-based games focused on testing and improving your reaction time, speed, and precision. No downloads, no sign-ups — just instant play.

The Creator

Built and maintained by Laxman Koirala. The goal is simple: make high-quality, fast-loading games that anyone can play for free on any device.

How It Works

All games run entirely in your browser using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Your high scores are stored locally on your device — nothing is sent to any server.

The site is supported by Google AdSense advertising. Ads allow the games to remain completely free for everyone. We appreciate your support.
