Response Time

Emails are checked regularly. Expect a reply within one to two business days for most enquiries. Bug reports that affect gameplay are prioritised. Feature requests are logged and considered for future development — a response may take longer or may come in the form of the feature being implemented.

What to Include in Your Message

To get the fastest and most useful reply, try to include the following in your email depending on the type of message:

  • Bug reports: The browser you were using (e.g. Chrome 124), the device type (desktop or mobile), what you were doing when the bug occurred, and what you expected to happen versus what actually happened. A screenshot is very helpful if the issue is visual.
  • Game suggestions: A brief description of the game concept, what makes it interesting as a reflex challenge, and any examples of similar games you have played elsewhere.
  • General feedback: Anything goes. Positive feedback is appreciated; critical feedback is even more useful.

About the Project

Reflex.GG is a solo project built in spare time. It does not have a support team, social media accounts, or customer service infrastructure. Direct email is the only supported contact channel. Responses are personal and genuine — not automated.

If you have not received a response within three business days, please feel free to send a follow-up. Occasionally emails land in spam or get missed.
